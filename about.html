Biography

Presley Chweneyagae was born in South Africa and rose to international fame with his breakout role in Tsotsi (2005), which won the Academy Award for Best Foreign Language Film. He has since starred in several films, TV shows, and stage plays, becoming a respected figure in South African entertainment.
